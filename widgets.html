Launching the app

On launching the app, you are requested to choose to continue as either a student or a custodian. If no option is selected after 5 seconds, the app assumes that you are blind and switches to another page.

Signing in as Custodian

If you login as a custodian you will be prompted to login or register and this will be followed by entering the details of the particular hostel you represent e.g rooms available, prices among others and then click the submit details option. If the custodian is also able to edit and update details. This can be done by edit and then clicking the submit details button where a message will be shown to the custooian when the details are submitted successfully.

Signing in as student

If you login as student, it will take you to the hostels page.When you click the details button, you are taken to a pagee with the details of that given hostel an they are dynamic depending on what the custodian through his page. When you click the loaction button, it takes yoou to page that automatically gets your location. Here there is a search bar (search hostels and location) where when searched and found you have to put your starting point and the hostel you are trying to go to. Then you will be required to choose the preffered means of transport which may be walking, motorcycle or vehicle and then a waypoint will be generated with the approximated tiome the journey will take.

Using app as a blind person

This is the one able to be used by the blind and a voice is output welcoming the user. From there, the blind user is given instructions forexample, if you want hostels in kikoni,tap the left corner of the screen.
