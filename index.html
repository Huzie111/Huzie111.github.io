Problem Identification

Many university students face a problem of getting a hostel to reside especially when the semester has already started due to less knowledge about the available hostels. Despite the general location of these hostels being known, students tend to only reach out to the popular ones which may be expensive yet there exist other affordable hostels they could access. Some of the students end up choosing hostels that are relatively far from the university and the to and from movement to the university is hectic especially in unfavourable weather. Some students apply for hostels with poor services like abrupt water shortage since there is no rating to show which hostels provide better services. The movement involved looking for a hostel is hectic as an individual may spend a whole week looking for and comparing standards and prices of various hostels.

Problem solution

The above problems are to be catered for by building a mobile app to provide all relevant information needed by the students looking for hostels. The information includes;

  • Availability of hostels and their prices.
  • Discrete services of a particular hostel which include;
    • Shuttle services
    • Reliable water and power supply
  • Location of the hostels
  • Booking fee for a given hostel
  • Location of the hostels
  • Contacts of custodians

The application is also to include a feature that enables use by users with visual impairment. These will recieve information using audio output and will enter input through touch screen by tapping a given corner on the screen.

How to interact with the application

On launching the application, the user will have to choose to continue either as a custodian or a student. If custodian, the user will have to login and then they will be able to make changes on details of a given hostel. If student, they will be taken to a page that has a map with hostels represented by unique symbols. Once a unique symbol is tapped, the details of the given hostel will be listed.
